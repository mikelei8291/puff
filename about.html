PUFF

  • PUFF 是一个由气压控制，可输出 MIDI 信号与 CV 信号的呼吸控制器
  • CV 输出不加过滤的原始气压值转换为的模拟信号
  • 特点是，两个旋钮分别物理可调阻力与阻尼
  • MIDI CC 控制与 CV 控制可以同时进行
  • PUFF is a breath controller that uses air pressure to generate MIDI and CV signals.
  • Analog signals converted from the raw, unfiltered air pressure value into the CV output.
  • It features two knobs that allow physical adjustment of air resistance and damping.
  • Both MIDI CC control and CV control can be used simultaneously.
